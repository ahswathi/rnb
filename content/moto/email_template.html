MOTORCYCLE ACCIDENT CLAIM

ACCIDENT MANAGEMENT

We deal with managing the non-faulty accident claim, which offers a best solution to get back you on road after your accident. Once we are confident that the accident was not your fault, we will forward the details for our company. We handle all types of vehicles. We will take care of getting your vehicle fixed and lend you a replacement vehicle to get around, we will deal with all the paperwork, and making sure you receive the full compensation to which you are entitled.

We offer a comprehensive service across the whole of the United Kingdom, taking care of everything from getting you back on the road in one of our free rental vans to setting up your medical appointments, scheduling any necessary physiotherapy sessions, and securing the highest possible settlement for your injuries. Our solicitor, who focus on cases involving traffic accidents, will make sure you receive the highest amount of compensation possible. The insurers for the third party are covering our legal bills.

As accident was not your fault the expenses are recovered from the third party’s insurance so our service costs you nothing. All expenses are recovered from the other party’s insurers. Because the accident was not your fault, we will recover any excesses your policy may contain because we deal directly with the other insurers your NO Claims Bonus is safe.
